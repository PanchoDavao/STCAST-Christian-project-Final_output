Empowering Future Office Administration Professionals

Santo Tomas College of Agriculture Sciences and Technology (STCAST) combines modern office management techniques with hands-on experience to prepare students for successful careers in office administration and business environments.

Our Mission

About STCAST

STCAST is a leading institution in office administration education. We are committed to empowering future office administration professionals with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to excel in the modern workplace.

Hands-on Learning

Field work, labs, and community projects ensure students graduate with real-world skills.

Industry Partnerships

Collaborations with local farms and agribusinesses help with internships and job placements.

Research & Innovation

Ongoing research in sustainable agriculture and agri-tech encourages student-led innovation.
